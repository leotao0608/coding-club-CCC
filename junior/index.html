Topics Covered in Junior CCC

According to the contest structure outlined by the CEMC (Centre for Education in Mathematics and Computing):

    • Questions 1 & 2
       Typically straightforward, involving basic loops, conditional statements, and simple arithmetic. 

    • Questions 3 & 4
       Introduce more complexity by combining loops, conditionals, and counting logic. 

    • Question 5
       Covers more advanced topics such as recursion, two-dimensional arrays, and efficient or insightful algorithmic
       approaches
